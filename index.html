about me

Hi I'm Sunny! I am a front-end webdeveloper from Berlin.

I started creating websites in late highschool and since then I have worked on webdev-projects not only in my spare time but also in a professional environment, where I built smaller showcases and took part in the design and marketing process.

Throughout my time I taught myself not only how to use basic Javascript, CSS and HTML, but also some react.js and vue.js, as well as having learnt Python a few years back.

One of my core qualities is my passion for learning. I regularly challenge myself to learn new skills, like 3D-Modelling, drawing or playing songs on my guitar and I'm always on the lookout for new technologies to make my work more efficient and easier.

gradient generator

A gradient generator taking in two color input values and coloring a varying amount of boxes

Boxes can be dynamically added and removed, changing steps of the gradient. The gradient automatically updates when elements are added, removed or colors are changed. There is a max and min amount of boxes (between 1 and 64) and gradient colors can be picked by clicking them.

A simple Todo list, where you can add, remove, and check-off todos
