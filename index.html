gradient generator

A gradient generator taking in two color input values and coloring a varying amount of boxes

Boxes can be dynamically added and removed, changing steps of the gradient. The gradient automatically updates when elements are added, removed or colors are changed. There is a max and min amount of boxes (between 1 and 64) and gradient colors can be picked by clicking them.
